# QSCM Content Vault

Use this vault to edit the site content.

- Posts live in `content/posts`.
- Static pages live in `content/pages`.
- Images and downloadable files live in `public/media` and are referenced in posts as `/media/file-name.ext`.
- Keep published `slug` values stable because they control the public URL.
- Use `status: "draft"` while writing, then change it to `status: "published"` when it is ready.
- Use a future `publishedAt` date to schedule a post.

New post and page templates are available from Obsidian's Templates command.
